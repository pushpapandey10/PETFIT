Why you’ll love it

The PetFit website and smart collar combine GPS tracking and heartbeat monitoring to ensure your pet’s safety and health. The collar allows real-time location tracking with geofencing alerts, notifying you if your pet leaves a designated safe zone. It also monitors your pet’s heart rate, providing early detection of unusual patterns or potential health concerns.

With instant alerts and veterinarian-ready reports, PetFit keeps your pet safe, healthy, and happy, giving you peace of mind.
